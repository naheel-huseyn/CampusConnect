    About Our College

    Introduction

    CampusConnect College of Engineering, located in Karachi and affiliated with University of Karachi, has been a hub of innovation and academic excellence since 1998. The college offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. With highly qualified faculty, modern laboratories, and industry-focused curricula, students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure. The vibrant campus life encourages participation in cultural, technical, and sports events, ensuring all-round development. Our strong alumni network and international collaborations provide global opportunities for students to excel and build successful careers.

    Achievements

    • Academic Excellence: Ranked among the top colleges affiliated with University of Karachi.
    • Technical Success: Winners at national hackathons & robotics championships.
    • Cultural Recognition: Annual cultural fest draws participation across the region.
    • Sports Victories: Champions in inter-college cricket and athletics.
    • Research & Innovation: Faculty and students publish in reputed journals.
    • Community Outreach: Active participation in social impact projects and drives.
    • Global Exposure: Student exchange programs with international universities.
    • Alumni Success: Graduates excelling in multinational companies worldwide.

    Technical Events

    Hackathon, TechFest, and Robotics Championship to promote innovation and creativity.

    Cultural Events

    Music Night, Annual Day, and Dance Competitions that showcase student talents.

    Sports Events

    Inter-college sports meets and tournaments to encourage teamwork and fitness.
